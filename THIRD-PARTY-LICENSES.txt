ConvertIA -- Third-Party Licenses
=================================

ConvertIA's own code is licensed under the MIT License (see the LICENSE file).
ConvertIA also bundles third-party conversion engines, each distributed under its
own license and invoked as a separate binary. For every bundled component, this file
records its name and version, its full license text, and -- for GPL/LGPL/AGPL
components -- a written offer of corresponding source (the pinned upstream tag and
the build recipe), honoring the project's copyleft obligations.
